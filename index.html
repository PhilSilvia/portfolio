Who am I?

I am a software and website developer living in rainy Marysville, WA.

Geeked from birth, I am very passionate about creating elegant and entertaining experiences for people to enjoy. That includes games, roleplaying settings and fun adventures for your computer or your tabletop.

I love working with others and collaborating to help bring everyone's ideas to the table and create a communal vision. Seeing someone's brainchild come to life is extremely fulfilling for me, whether it is mine or someone else's!

Okay, but what have I done?

I graduated from the University of New Mexico in 2009 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.

Since then I have pursued a variety of different roles over the next fifteen years.

  1. I worked for a couple years as a systems analyst at a government contracting company.
  2. I was the owner and operator of a game store that sold tabletop card games, board games, and miniature games for about five years.
  3. I was a full-time homemaker on-and-off for a few years
  4. A shift supervisor at my local department store as well.

Having had many different roles and experiences I'm very excited to return to my roots as a programmer and developer. I have been working hard to catch up on new techniques and languages, to make sure I can combine a unique perspective and management skills with solid programming fundamentals.

I offer a lot so if you are looking for a developer with a unique combination of new and old experience, I would love to work with you and your team to create novel programming solutions.
